Hello,
I'm Liz Wishaw

As a visual journalist, my 20-year career in journalism has taken me across the United States with stops in Detroit, Dallas and Tacoma.

A passion for storytelling and world travel led me into journalism — and if you ask my mom, as a little kid, I always asked "Why?".
A path in print design and copy editing led to advancement into production management. These positions have required news judgement, organization, research and collaboration. As the news industry shifted, so did I.

Other interests

Pop culture, memoirs, a morning run to area parks is how I spend my time outside the office. Good food, Pacific Northwest wines, vegetarian-pescatarian recipes and travel photography fill the rest of the time.
